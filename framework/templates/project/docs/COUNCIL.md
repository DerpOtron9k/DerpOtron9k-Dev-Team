## Delivery Ring

The active delivery-side support seats installed in `.codex/agents`:

__DELIVERY_SEATS__

These seats are advisory and operational. They support backlog hygiene, sprint structure, and acceptance clarity. They do not vote on architecture and do not override the user as Product Owner.

## Kickoff Readiness

- a selected architecture council
- a confirmed delivery ring
- confirmed MCP staples or an explicit setup gap for Serena and Context7
- continuity files for every active seat
- an accepted initial sprint or a recommendation to refine backlog first
